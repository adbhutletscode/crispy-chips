Our Journey

2005

The Beginning

Crispy Chips started in the Smith family kitchen, with a simple recipe and a passion for perfect potato chips.

2008

First Store

We opened our first small production facility and began selling to local grocery stores and markets.

2012

Expansion

Growing demand led to expanded production and distribution across the state, with new flavors added to our lineup.

2015

Organic Line

We launched our organic line of chips, committing to sustainable farming practices and eco-friendly packaging.

2020

Online Store

We embraced e-commerce, making our chips available nationwide through our online store.

Today

Continuing Innovation

We continue to innovate with new flavors and products while maintaining our commitment to quality and sustainability.
